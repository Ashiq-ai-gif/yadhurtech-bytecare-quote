Comprehensive Platform Flow

Visualizing the seamless journey from customer service request to resolution and billing across all modules.

Imagine a multi-lane flowchart vividly detailing each step: Customer App (raising request) → Admin Panel (assignment) → Staff App (service & update) → Customer App (tracking & payment).

1. Customer Initiates Service

Via Customer Mobile App: User registers/logs in, raises service request (type, device, issue details, images), selects address/date, and receives auto Ticket ID.

2. Admin Review & Assignment

Via Admin Dashboard: New booking appears. Admin reviews, approves/rejects/reschedules, and assigns the ticket to an available technician via the Staff App.

3. Technician Action & Updates

Via Internal Staff App: Technician logs in, accepts assigned ticket, views details, updates service status (In Progress, Completed), and communicates with the customer.

4. Real-time Customer Tracking

Via Customer Mobile App: Customer receives push/SMS/WhatsApp notifications on status changes, tracks technician progress, and views service updates.

5. Billing & Payment

Via Admin Dashboard: Admin generates invoice post-completion. Via Customer Mobile App: Customer makes online payment (UPI, Net Banking, QR Code) and downloads PDF invoice.

6. AMC & Feedback Loop

Via Admin Dashboard: AMC tracking and reminders set. Via Customer Mobile App: Customer provides service feedback and ratings, influencing future technician assignments.
